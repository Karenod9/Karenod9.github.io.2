ABOUT
THE MORGAN
GUESTHOUSE

The Morgan is a family run guesthouse nestled in the heart of Dublin city center. The Morgan Guesthouse provides a calm oasis in which to retreat to from the bustling city. For more than 15 years The Morgan Guesthouse has been providing a warm and friendly welcome to travellers from all over the world. We pride ourselves in ensuring that every guest is made to feel like they are staying in a home away from home.
