WELCOME TO
THE MORGAN

The Morgan a family run guesthouse, located, in the heart of the city, is here to provide a relaxing and luxurious stay in the iconic IFSC area of Dublin. In operation for over 15 years The Morgan is well versed in anticipating your needs. With an onsite bar and restaurant, we provide a hotel experience but at a fraction of the cost.

THE MORGAN
Dublin

The Morgan Guesthouse, which is located in the iconic IFSC area of Dublin City Center is the perfect location for a short or long getaway to Dublin, Ireland. The location makes it a perfect stopping point for those attending various concerts in the Three Arena, Bord Gas Theatre or Abbey Theatre. It's also only a stones throw away from the Convention Centre and EPIC - The Irish Emigration Museum. The LUAS which is right outside the front door will take you to all parts of the city such as O'Connell Street, Stephens Green and the iconic Grafton Street shopping district.
